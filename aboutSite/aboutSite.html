About This Website

This website was designed and coded by Ethan Vu. All pictures were also taken by him except for the circular picture on the home page. That's a shot of myself giving my sister a piggyback ride many years ago taken by a friend.

I created this website with 2 goals in mind.

The first was to practice my coding skills, specifically in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Because of this, I really wanted to add in a lot of fancy stuff like the transitioning backgrounds on the home page and my flying resume on the about me page.

The second was to practice my articulation skills. 90% of being smart is getting the point across clearly and interestingly. Thinking back, I've told about 3 good stories in my entire life. Every other story I tell has me stuttering and digging for the right words. This website will give me a platform to practice writing skills.

A person writes his best when he writes about things that he is passionate about. So I'll be talking about video games and baseball.

ethanvu.github.io will essentially be my personal blog. At least for now. I have some other ideas that I could use my website for.

By the way, the background image for this page is a shot I took of Jupiter, the Moon, and the ISS on April 18, 2016 @ 8:31 PM EST over Blacksburg, VA. I call this work of art, "Space Triangle". It's low res because it's a screen shot of a higher res video that I took of the event, but I love this picture too much to not use it.
